Whats is STEAM?

STEAM means, Science Technology Engineering the Art and Mathematics. In our daily life, in all sectors we need people that knows STEAM.

What we can do to empower women in STEAM?

1 - Education and Awareness:
Encourage and support girls in pursuing STEAM subjects from an early age.
Raise awareness about the contributions of women in STEAM fields through educational programs and events.

2 - Role Models and Mentorship:
Showcase successful women in STEAM as role models to inspire young girls.
Establish mentorship programs connecting experienced women in STEAM with those entering the field.

3 - Address Gender Bias:
Promote awareness and education about unconscious bias in the workplace and academia.
Implement policies that ensure fair evaluation and promotion practices.

4 - Inclusive Work Environments:
Foster inclusive and diverse work environments that value and celebrate differences.
Implement flexible work arrangements to accommodate family responsibilities.

5 - Networking Opportunities:
Create networking events and conferences specifically focused on women in STEAM.
Support the formation of women's groups and professional organizations in STEAM fields.

6 - Scholarships and Funding:
Establish scholarships and funding opportunities specifically for women pursuing STEAM education and careers.
Advocate for equal opportunities for research funding and resources.

7 - Educational Outreach:
Develop outreach programs to schools and communities to generate interest in STEAM among girls.
Support initiatives that provide STEAM resources and education to underprivileged communities.

8 - Promote Gender Diversity in Leadership:
Encourage the inclusion of women in leadership roles within STEAM organizations.
Advocate for diversity and inclusion policies at all levels of STEAM institutions.

9 - Challenge Stereotypes:
Challenge and debunk stereotypes about gender roles in STEAM through media and educational campaigns.
Encourage diverse portrayals of scientists and engineers in the media.

10 - Support Return-to-Work Programs:
Implement programs that support women who have taken career breaks to re-enter the workforce.
Provide opportunities for ongoing professional development.

11 - Collaboration and Partnerships:
Encourage collaboration between industry, academia, and government to address gender disparities in STEAM.
Partner with organizations that actively promote gender diversity and inclusion.

12 - Advocate for Policy Changes:
Advocate for policies that promote gender equality in STEAM education and workplaces.
Support initiatives that address sySTEAMic barriers to women's participation in STEAM.
